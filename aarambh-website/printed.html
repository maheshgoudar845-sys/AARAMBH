How to care for DTF prints

Always turn your printed tee inside out before washing. Use cold water (max 30°C) on a gentle cycle. Avoid direct sunlight when drying. Do not iron directly on the print — use a cloth layer if needed. Following these steps keeps your print vibrant wash after wash.
